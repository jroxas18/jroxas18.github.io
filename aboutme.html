My Story...

I was born on raised on the beautifl island of Sainpan, a tiny US territory in the pacific in the Northern Marianas Islands. As a child, I had many ambitions. I told my Mom that I wanted to be a lot of things, a doctor, a civil engineer, an architect, and even a fire truck, yes a fire truck. I used to enjoy drawing as a child and often practiced it. It eventually came to a point where I was capable of perfectly copying Toy Story characters. However I eventually got bored of it and just lost my talent for it. I spent my days mostly with my friends at their house, playing video games, playing tag, going out to watch movies, and a lot of sleepovers. We would see each other six to seven days a week. Looking back, the friends I made were always tech savy people. They were either gamers, ITs, one was a technical director for Theatre club, and other people who liked computers. I guess they influenced me into pursuing a degree in Information Systems. They always talked about tech stuff and eventually I became interested as well.
